## Recommended Tools

### For Images
- **Midjourney** - Best for abstract tech visuals
- **DALL-E 3** - Good for clean illustrations
- **Leonardo AI** - Good for consistent style
- **Stable Diffusion** - Free, customizable

### For Videos
- **Runway Gen-3** - AI video generation
- **Pika Labs** - Motion from images
- **Kling AI** - Longer form videos
- **Luma Dream Machine** - Cinematic AI video
- **After Effects + Lottie** - Motion graphics
